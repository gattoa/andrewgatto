Designing to uplift

I’m a passionate Designer and Product Leader dedicated to crafting high-quality, accessible design solutions that provide undeniable value.

I take a holistic approach that bridges business, design, and technology to create solutions that aren’t just visually appealing, but truly meaningful. At my core, I believe quality isn’t a luxury—it’s something every person deserves.

Currently, I lead Product and Design at Khaos Studio.

I founded Simple. Quality. UX & Product Design to help others on their design journey and I work with DesignLab to mentor the next generation of designers.

My previous career stops include Warner Bros. Discovery (Food Network, HGTV), SiriusXM/Pandora, BDO USA and Grant Thornton LLP.
